If inflation was already re-accelerating, we have stagflation confirmed — rising prices + slowing growth + a massive energy shock on top. The Fed would be trapped.

A weakening dollar during a war suggests markets are losing confidence in US economic management. The combination of war costs + stagflation + fiscal strain is weighing on the greenback.

🧠 Why $115 Oil Changes Everything

🛢 The Oil Price Isn't Just a Commodity Number — It's a Tax on the Entire Economy

Every $10 increase in oil adds roughly +0.3% to headline CPI and subtracts ~0.1% from GDP growth. Oil went from $67 to $115 in 10 days — that's a ~$48 increase, implying:

+1.4% added to inflation (on top of whatever February CPI shows)
-0.5% drag on GDP growth
Average US gas price heading toward $5+/gallon (from ~$3.20 pre-war)

Wednesday's CPI is the hinge point: it measures FEBRUARY — before the oil shock. If inflation was ALREADY rising, the Fed is trapped. They can't cut rates (inflation too high) and can't raise rates (economy weakening). That's stagflation — the worst macro scenario.

The Russell 2000 (-3.72%, worst performer) tells the real story. Small caps are domestic-focused companies that can't hedge oil costs or pass them through like megacaps. They're the canary in the coal mine for a US recession.

🛢 Oil → CPI Pipeline

Oil at $115 feeds directly into Wednesday's CPI print. Goldman calls it "the most important print of the year." If inflation was already rising before this shock, stagflation is confirmed. Every $10 rise in oil adds ~0.3% to headline CPI within 2 months.

🏭 Refinery Damage = Months

Destroyed refineries and storage facilities take months to repair — even a ceasefire tomorrow won't bring oil prices back quickly. The supply destruction is physical and real. Iran oil infrastructure is actively burning.

📉 Small Caps Crushed

Russell 2000 down -3.72% — hardest hit index by far. Small caps are the most vulnerable to the recession + oil shock combination. These companies can't hedge energy costs like megacaps. The canary is choking.

🏦 The Fed Trap

Powell faces an impossible choice: cut rates (inflation surges higher) or hold rates (economy weakens into recession). Classic stagflation trap. The FOMC decision on Mar 17-18 will define the next quarter.

🔮 Three Scenarios

🕊 CEASEFIRE
Oil drops ~30% ($80-85 range). Markets rally 5-8%. VIX collapses. But refinery damage caps recovery speed. Best case: 2-3 month normalization.
🔥 ESCALATION
Oil $140+. S&P drops 15-20% from highs. Recession confirmed. Iran retaliates against Saudi infrastructure. Global energy crisis. Fed forced into emergency action.
⚖ STALEMATE
Oil range-bound $100-120. Markets grind lower with high volatility. Slow-burn stagflation. Fed holds rates. Most likely scenario for next 2-4 weeks.

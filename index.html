🧠 What This Means

📈
The Reversal Is Real — But Fragile
The massive oil reversal ($100+ → $85) shows how binary this market is: any ceasefire signal = instant relief rally. Trump's "very complete" comment was the first real wind-down signal in 10 days. The S&P going from -2% to +0.83% intraday is historically bullish — if it holds.
⚠️
Infrastructure Damage Is Permanent
Even with an immediate ceasefire, destroyed refineries take months to rebuild. Iraq at -70% output doesn't snap back. Qatar LNG offline doesn't restart overnight. Oil at $85 is still +27% from pre-war levels. The damage is baked in.
🎯
Wednesday CPI Is Still The Main Event
February CPI measures prices before oil went from $67 to $100+. If inflation was already climbing from tariffs alone, adding an energy shock on top = stagflation confirmed. If CPI comes in soft, the Fed has room to cut and markets rally hard. The entire week pivots on that single print.
🌐
Two Scenarios Now
Bull case: Trump follows through, war winds down in weeks, oil retreats to $70s, soft CPI on Wednesday → relief rally for ages. Bear case: "Very complete" is bluster, new Supreme Leader escalates, hot CPI → oil back to $100+, stagflation, and the Fed is trapped. We'll know by Wednesday.
